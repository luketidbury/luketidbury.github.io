Luke Tidbury

Hello, I am Luke Tidbury, here is my website.

"From the artist who has produced ‘Adrift’ and ‘Sunrise’, Luke Tidbury is an ambitious young artist who creates tracks that effortlessly conveys heartfelt and sentimental emotions to listeners all around the world. 
The 17 year old native to Portsmouth, UK started his music production journey in 2016 after being heavily influenced by artists such as Solace, Quadeca & Cookin Soul. Despite the producer’s impressive age he has since accomplished achievements including being added on the ‘Focus Flow’ editorial in November of 2022 and acquiring an awe-inspiring number of 500k streams thanks to his release ‘Eudaemonia’ on all streaming platforms. The producer currently attends college in Portsmouth to pursue his studies in music production and to increase his knowledge in the music market. It is evident the musician is determined to create the best music possible for his listeners as he dearly appreciates networking, collaborating and receiving feedback from others. Luke Tidbury’s journey in music is just starting as the young artist has accomplished so much in such a short period of time. He expresses that he is ‘very grateful for the opportunity to do what I love, and to hopefully help any of my listener’s out there with my music’." 

- leah julia
